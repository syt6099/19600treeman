About Treeman 19600 Team

The treeman 19600 team represents YK Pao High School, and have been one of the strongest teams in Shanghai, China.

Joining the FIRST Tech Challenge competition for over 8 years, Treeman 19600 team has won a range of awards. Check out our achievements here.

We have excellent members participating in engineering projects, programming projects, outreaching and project managing.

We explore the field of knowledge in the FTC challenge. We are excited to encounter and challenge the obstacles that come up in this season!
